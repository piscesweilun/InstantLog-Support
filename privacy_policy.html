Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how InstantLog ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Data Collection and Usage

We believe in privacy by design. Most of your data is stored locally on your device.

Local Data

Your timestamps, notes, and tags are stored locally on your device using on-device database technologies. We do not transmit this personal data to our own servers.

Third-Party Services (Advertising)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads, including:

  • Device Identifiers (e.g., IDFA)
  • Usage Data (e.g., crash logs, diagnostics)
  • Approximate Location

For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

2. App Permissions

  • Siri & Shortcuts: Used solely to allow you to create logs via voice commands or shortcuts.
  • Notifications: Used to provide reminders if you set them (optional).

3. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not knowingly directed to children under the age of 4. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

piscesweilun.studio@gmail.com

1. 資料收集與使用

我們重視您的隱私，絕大多數的功能資料皆儲存於您的裝置端。

本機資料

您的時間戳記、備註與標籤等資料，皆使用裝置端的資料庫技術儲存於您的手機中。我們不會將這些個人記錄傳送到我們的伺服器。

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I delete a record?

Swipe left on any record in the History view to reveal the delete button.

2. How does Smart Auto-Fill work?

InstantLog analyzes your past records. If you often log "Lunch" at 12:00 PM, the app will suggest "Lunch" when you stamp around that time again. You can also set explicit rules in Settings.

3. Does the app work without internet?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. An internet connection is only required for backing up data or loading ads.

4. How do I backup my data?

Go to Settings > Data Management > Backup & Restore. This is a Premium feature that allows you to export your data for safekeeping.

常見問題 (FAQ)

1. 如何刪除紀錄？

在「歷史紀錄」頁面中，將任何一筆紀錄「向左滑動」，即可看到刪除按鈕。

2. 智慧自動填寫是如何運作的？

InstantLog 會分析您過去的紀錄習慣。例如，若您經常在中午 12 點記錄「午餐」，下次在這個時間打卡時，App 便會自動建議「午餐」。您也可以在「設定」中自行建立規則。

3. 沒有網路可以使用嗎？

可以！所有資料都儲存在您的手機內部。只有在備份資料或載入廣告時才需要網路連線。

4. 如何備份資料？

請前往 設定 > 資料管理 > 備份與還原。這是一項進階功能，讓您可以匯出資料以防止遺失。
